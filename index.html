Technical Specification FAQ

Deep dive into our AI architecture and data security protocols.

How does non-intrusive load monitoring (NILM) work?

Our proprietary NILM algorithms analyze high-frequency current and voltage signatures. By identifying the unique "electrical fingerprint" of devices—like a compressor starting in an AC or the pulse-width modulation of an EV charger—we can separate total house load into individual appliance categories.

What is the data latency for grid-side analytics?

Bidgely supports both batch processing (24-hour intervals) and near real-time streams via HAN (Home Area Network) devices, providing insights in as little as 5-15 minute intervals for active demand response events.

Is the platform SOC-2 Compliant?

Yes. Data privacy is our cornerstone. All PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256. We never sell consumer data; our models are built for utility-consumer optimization only.

Can Bidgely detect behind-the-meter Solar and Battery storage?

Absolutely. Our AI distinguishes between negative load (generation) and reduced consumption, allowing utilities to map true DER (Distributed Energy Resource) penetration across the distribution transformer level.
